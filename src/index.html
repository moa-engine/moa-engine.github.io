Metasearch Engine,
Respect Your Privacy

MOA is a free and open-source meta-search engine. This application is designed with the aim of simplicity and usability for the general public, providing good and usable results while ensuring privacy, transparency, and freedom on the internet.

Privacy and Security:MOA prioritizes user privacy and does not collect any personal data from users. Your activities will not be tracked or stored, ensuring complete anonymity and security.

Decentralized:Anyone can serve MOA on their server for their family, all people, or even just for themselves. You can even simply run it on your computer.

Ease of Use for All

MOA's clean and intuitive UI prioritizes user privacy and ease of use, ensuring a safe and enjoyable browsing experience.

Transparent

The development of MOA is transparent on GitHub and we will document its development process as much as possible.

Plugins and Themes

MOA supports various plugins. Anyone can write a new plugin, theme, or even a better search engine according to their needs.

Fully Supported Perso-ArabicScript

Get complete and comprehensive support for Farsi(Persian), Arabic, Urdu and etc

Contribute

This is your search engine too, contribute and improve it
